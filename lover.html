Lover

Lover by Taylor Swift was released on August 23, 2019, as her seventh studio album. Lover was the singers first album released under Republic Records after leaving her previous record label of thirteen years, Big Machine Records.

Lover features a track list of 18 songs and is 1 hour and 1 minute in length. The album is classified under the pop and electro-pop genres.
