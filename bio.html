The Life of Taylor Swift

Taylor 'Alison' Swift is an acclaimed American singer-songwriter who was born on December 13, 1989 in West Reading, Pennsylvania USA.

Swift's career began in 2003 when the singer-songwriter moved to Nashville, Tennessee where she eventually signed with Big Machine Records to follow her dream of becoming a country musician.

Swift has grown an immense amount since her country days, and is now considered one of the biggest stars in the world. Throughout her career, Swift has released 11 studio albums, gone on 6 concert tours including the highest-grossing concert tour of all time - The Eras Tour, and received dozens of awards and accolades for her music.

Swift's career is still going strong after 21 years in the industry and the singer-songwriter is bigger than ever before with a constantly-growing fanbase of millions of "Swifties" from all over the world.
