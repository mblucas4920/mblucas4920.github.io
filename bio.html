The Life of Taylor Swift

Taylor 'Alison' Swift was born on December 13, 1989.


Career Timeline

2005

Signed with Big Machine Records

In 2005, Taylor Swift signed a record
deal with record label "Big Machine Records" at the age of 16.

2006

Released self-titled debut album, Taylor Swift.

2008

Released sophomore album, Fearless.

2009

Received first award for “Best Music Video” at the 2009 VMAs

At the 2009 MTV Video Music Awards Swift won the award for
"Best Music Video" with her music video for the song
"You Belong With Me" from the singers sophomore album "Fearless."

This was signifigant not just because it marked Swift's first
main-stream award, but also because while accepting the award,
rapper Kanye West came on stage and interrupted the at-the-time
19 year old girl.

This incident garnered critisisms of West, and support for Swift,
and marked the beginning of what would become a years long fued
between the two.

2010

Released third studio album, Speak Now.

Received first awards for…

  • “Album of the Year” (“Fearless”)
  • “Best Country Female Performance” (“White Horse”)
  • "Best Country Song" (“White Horse”)
  • “Best Country Album” (“Fearless”) at the 52nd Annual
    Grammy Awards Show.

2012

Released fourth studio album, Red.

2014

Released fifth studio album, 1989.

2017

Released sixth studio album, reputation.

2018

Contract with Big Machine Records expires; Swift signs with Republic Records

2019

Released seventh studio album Lover. This was her first album under Republic Records

Entered dispute with former record label, Big Machine Records, over the masters of her first six studio albums (“Taylor Swift,” “Fearless,” “Speak Now,” “Red,” “1989,” and “Reputation”)

Received award for “Artist of the Decade” at the American Music Awards

2020

Released eighth and ninth studio albums, folklore and evermore.

2021

Released first and second re-recorded albums, Fearless(Taylors Version) and Red(Taylors Version)

2022

Released tenth studio album, Midnights.

Announced 'The Eras Tour.'

2023

Released third and fourth re-recorded albums, Speak Now(Taylors Version) and 1989(Taylors Version)

Began 'The Eras Tour.'

2024

Released eleventh studio album, The Tortured Poets Department.
