1989

1989 by Taylor Swift was released October 27, 2014 as her fifth studio album under Big Machine Records. 1989 was the fourth album to be re-released as "1989 (Taylors Version)" by the singer/songwriter under new record label Republic Records on October 27, 2023.

1989 originally featured a track list of 16 songs and was 1 hour and 1 minute in length, but 1989 (Taylors Version) increased the track list to 21 songs and the length to 1 hour and 17 minutes. The album is classified under the pop genre.
