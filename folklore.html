folklore

Folklore (stylized as folklore) by Taylor Swift was released on July 24, 2020, as her eighth studio album. Folklore was the singers second album released under Republic Records after leaving her previous record label of thirteen years, Big Machine Records.

folklore features a track list of 17 songs and is 1 hour and 17 minutes in length. The album is classified under the indie-folk and alternative-rock genres. folklore is critically acclaimed and won the Album of the Year award at the 2021 Grammy Music Awards.
