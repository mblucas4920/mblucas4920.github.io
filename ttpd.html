The Tortured Poets Department

The Tortured Poets Department by Taylor Swift was released April 19, 2024 as her eleventh studio album. The album was also the singers fifth album released under Republic Records after leaving her previous record label of thirteen years, Big Machine Records.

The Tortured Poets Department features a tracklist of 31 songs and is 2 hours and 2 minutes in length. The album is classified under the pop, synth-pop, folk-pop, and chamber pop genres.
