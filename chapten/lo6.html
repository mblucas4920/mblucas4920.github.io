Types of Rating Errors

Similar-to-Me Error

  • Overrating people that seem similar
  • People tend to give higher evaluation scores to people they consider similar to themselves

Contrast Error

  • Underrating people that seem different

Halo Error

  • Overrating based on one good quality

Horns Error

  • Underrating based on one poor quality

Errors in Distribution

  • Overusing part of a rating scale
  • Leniency is when the reviewer rates everyone near the top
  • Strictness is when the reviewer favors lower rankings
  • Central tendency is when the reviewer puts everyone near the middle of the scale
