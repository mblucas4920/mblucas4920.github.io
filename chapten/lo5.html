Main Sources of Performance Information

Managers: The most used source of performance information
Peers: Excellent source of information about performance in a job where a supervisor does not often observe the employee
Subordinates: The people reporting to the managerâ€”often have the best chance to see how well a manager treats employees
Self: A common approach is to have employees evaluate their own performance before the feedback session. This activity gets employees thinking about their performance.
Customers Services are often produced and consumed on the spot, so the customer is often the only person who directly observes the service performance and may be the best source of performance information.
