USC Swifties

Welcome to the hub for Taylor Swift Fans (Swifties) attending school at the University of South Carolina. The world is big and hard to navigate, especially for students leaving home for the first time, but through Taylor Swift and her music fans can find communities of like-minded souls wherever they go. Our website’s goal is to create A ‘Place in This World’ where the Swifties of USC never go out of ‘Style’!
