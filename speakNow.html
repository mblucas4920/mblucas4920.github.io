Speak Now

Speak Now by Taylor Swift was released October 10, 2010 as her third studio album under Big Machine Records. Speak Now was the third album to be re-released as "Speak Now (Taylors Version)" by the singer/songwriter under new record label Republic Records on July 7th, 2023.

Speak Now originally featured a track list of 17 songs and was 1 hour and 19 minutes in length, but Speak Now (Taylors Version) increased the track list to 22 songs and the length to 1 hour and 44 minutes. The album is classified under the pop-rock and country-pop genres.

Pitchfork: 8.2/10
