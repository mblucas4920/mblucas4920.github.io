Fearless

Fearless by Taylor Swift was released November 11, 2008 as her second studio album under Big Machine Records. Fearless was also the first album to be re-released as "Fearless (Taylors Version)" by the singer/songwriter under new record label Republic Records on April 9, 2021.

Fearless originally featured a track list of 18 songs and was 1 hour and 15 minutes in length, but Fearless (Taylors Version) increased the track list to 26 songs and the length to 1 hour and 56 minutes. The album is classified under the country-pop genre.
