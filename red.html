Red

Red by Taylor Swift was released October 22, 2012 as her fourth studio album under Big Machine Records. Red was the second album to be re-released as "Red (Taylors Version)" by the singer/songwriter under new record label Republic Records on November 12, 2021.

Red originally featured a track list of 19 songs and was 1 hour and 17 minutes in length, but Red (Taylors Version) increased the track list to 29 songs and the length to 2 hour and 5 minutes. The album is classified under the pop, country-pop, and soft-rock genres.
