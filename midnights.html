Midnights

Midnights by Taylor Swift was released on October 21, 2022, as her tenth studio album. Midnights was the singers fourth album released under Republic Records after leaving her previous record label of thirteen years, Big Machine Records.

Midnights features a track list of 20 songs and is 1 hour and 9 minutes in length. The album is classified under the pop, synth-pop, and electro-pop genres.
